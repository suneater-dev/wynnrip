# Background Music Setup

To add cemetery/atmospheric music to your website:

## 1. Find Cemetery Music

Look for royalty-free dark/eerie/cemetery music from:
- **YouTube Audio Library** (Free)
- **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com/music/)
- **Free Music Archive** (https://freemusicarchive.org/)
- **Incompetech** (https://incompetech.com/)

Search terms: "dark ambient", "horror atmosphere", "cemetery", "eerie music", "dark drone"

## 2. Add Your Music File

1. Download your chosen music file
2. Name it `cemetery-music.mp3` (or update the filename in `src/App.jsx`)
3. Place it in this folder: `public/audio/cemetery-music.mp3`

## 3. Supported Formats

- `.mp3` (recommended for best compatibility)
- `.wav`
- `.ogg`

## 4. Music Player Features

The music player includes:
- ✅ Play/Pause button (bottom right corner)
- ✅ Mute/Unmute toggle
- ✅ Loops continuously
- ✅ Set to 30% volume by default
- ✅ Glowing green themed controls

## Quick Free Option

**Recommended:** Search YouTube for "dark ambient cemetery music royalty free" and download using a YouTube to MP3 converter, or use:
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/music/search/dark%20ambient/
- Free Music Archive: https://freemusicarchive.org/search?adv=1&quicksearch=dark+ambient

Make sure the music is royalty-free or you have the rights to use it!
